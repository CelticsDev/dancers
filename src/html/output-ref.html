Fun Facts:

Why did you choose to become a Boston Celtics Dancer?

I always loved the style of dance this team performs. I love how we can perform a sassy jazz routine and switch it up and perform a hardcore hip-hop dance.

What are you most excited about for the upcoming Celtics season?

I am most excited to perform in front of the amazing Boston fans and becoming a part of the Boston Celtics family.

What's the best thing about Boston fans?

They are loud! Every time they cheer it gets me pumped up. I'm also Puerto Rican, and my family and I are very loud. So I feel right at home! 

What's your favorite pre-game/practice snack?

I wouldn't call coffee a snack, but it's a must before every rehearsal and game.

What would your teammates say your superlative is?

Most likely to be singing.

What are your off-season hobbies?

I love to perform, so I try to do that as much as possible whether it's dancing, singing, acting or modeling. I enjoy them all!

If you could take a dream vacation anywhere, where would it be?

Greece.

If you could invite any three famous people to dinner, who would you invite?

Ellen DeGeneres, Carrie Underwood and Jimmy Fallon.

If you were a superhero what super power would you have?

I would be able to teleport, so I would never have to sit in traffic again.

If you had a time machine, which decade would you choose to go to and why?

I would say the 80's because I can see myself rocking the big hair, neon clothes and blasting Madonna.
